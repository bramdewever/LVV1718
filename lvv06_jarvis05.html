Paulo Freire

    • Permanent critical approach to reality in order to discover it and discover the myths that deceive us and help to maintain the oppressing dehumanizing structures
    • Term no longer used

Paulo Freire

  • No education can be neutral since the culture of the opressed is in opposition to that of the elite
  • Education is always a political process since it is a social institution controlled by the social and political processes, which almost automatically ensure that social pressures are brought to bear on learners in order to make them conform to what is socially prescribed in both cognitive and behavioural terms

Jack Mezirow

  • Constructions of reality: “perspectives"
    • Getransformeerd wanneer ze niet in overeenstemming zijn met ervaringen [reflectie]
    • 10 stages
    • 7 niveaus van reflectie

ten stages

  • a disorienting dillemma
  • self-examination
  • critical assessment and a sense of alienation
  • relating discontent to the experiences of others
  • exploring options for new ways of acting

ten stages

  • building confidence in new ways of behaving
  • planning a course of action
  • acquiring knowledge in order to implement plans
  • expirimenting with new roles
  • reintegration into society
