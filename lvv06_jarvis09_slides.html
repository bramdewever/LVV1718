Stephen Brookfield

  • Practice of teaching
  • Kritische denkers:
    • Zowel studenten als leraren
  • “teaching is of uttermost importance as ‘the instigator of significant learning’...” (Jarvis, 2010, p. 182)
  • Autonomie van de lerende
  • Tegemoet komen aan noden

Jerome S. Bruner

  • Probleemoplosser = ultieme einddoel
  • “... any instruction that is given in school should be regarded as having an intermediate as well as long-term aim, the latter being that the learner should become a self-sufficient problem solver.” (Jarvis, 2010, p. 184)

John Dewey

  • Progressive education
  • “... genuine education must come through experience.” (Jarvis, 2010, p. 186)
  • Scientific method:
    • problem
    • hypothesis
    • empirical evidence
  • Facilitative learning

Paulo Freire

  1. Going to those who have a learning need and listening to them
  2. Encourage learners to participate in dialogue and problematize reality
  3. Educator and learner have no mutually distinct roles

Ivan Illich

  • De-institutionalization
  • De-schooling
  • Informal
  • Learning networks
  • Free university system in USA
  • University of the Third Age in UK
  • Working outside of the intitutional framework

Malcolm Knowles

  • Sixteen principles of teaching
  • Seven (eight!!!) stages in process of teaching
  • Diagnosis: end-state, present level, gap
  • Zie ook hoofdstukken van Knowles in de reader

Parker Palmer

  • “We teach who we are”
  • Angst
  • Education as a spiritual journey
  • Teaching is an art, rather then a science
  • Trust in relationship to acknowledge own [teacher] fear and recognize their [students] fear.
